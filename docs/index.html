Crew tracking that works when cell doesn't.

Innawoods is an off-grid mapping app for the people you go into the woods with. End-to-end encrypted positions, chat, pins, and routes. LoRa-mesh support so you stay connected without a tower.

What it does

  • Live map of your crew's positions, fully offline-capable
  • End-to-end encrypted chat, route drawing, and pin sharing
  • Pairs with Meshtastic-firmware LoRa radios for true off-grid mesh
  • Region-downloadable vector basemaps + terrain shading
  • Dead-man's switch alerts if a crew member goes silent

What it doesn't do

  • Doesn't ask for your name, email, phone, or password
  • Doesn't run analytics or share data with anyone
  • Doesn't see your locations, chat, or party content — server only holds ciphertext
